# Appointments and Registration

## About the organization
Surrey Food Bank provides food assistance services to individuals and families in the community.

## About the project
This project focuses on improving the appointment booking and registration process used by the food bank. The intent is to streamline workflows for both clients and staff.

## Key features or deliverables
- Appointment control
- Client registration
- Check-in support

Meetings are available as needed.
