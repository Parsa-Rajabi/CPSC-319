# New CMS

## About the organization
Aunt Leahs Place is a nonprofit organization that supports youth and families.

## About the project
The project involves creating a new content management system to replace existing tools. The aim is to improve usability, reporting, and content management while meeting privacy and compliance requirements.

## Key features or deliverables
- Easy to update content
- Easy to generate reports
- Easy to manage users and permissions

## Constraints and considerations
The system must meet federal privacy standards and ensure secure handling of sensitive data.

## Timeline and availability
The organization is willing to meet every two weeks.
